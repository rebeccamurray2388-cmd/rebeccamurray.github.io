Projects

numbered case studies

01 — Managed Onboarding (Enterprise Activation System)

Program Owner • System Design • Delivery Ops
  • Context: Enterprise customers needed a structured activation path to reach value quickly.
  • Problem: Quality and timelines varied across customers and delivery teams.
  • Approach: Built end-to-end onboarding system: intake → milestones → enablement → QA gates.
  • Outcome: Delivered 200+ onboardings personally; scaled the system to 600+ via program design.

02 — Authorized Trainer Rollout (Badging + LMS Pathway)

Enablement Systems • Governance • Scale
  • Context: Partner-led delivery needed to scale without degrading the customer experience.
  • Problem: No consistent readiness standards, eligibility, or quality controls.
  • Approach: Built eligibility criteria, badging framework, and LMS pathways with clear governance.
  • Outcome: Increased partner delivery capacity while maintaining activation standards and program integrity.

03 — GSI High-Velocity Enablement Programs

Training Ops • Partner Enablement • Metrics
  • Context: Global SIs required rapid ramp across regions to meet enterprise demand.
  • Problem: Readiness signals and consistency were hard to measure at scale.
  • Approach: Designed cohort enablement + readiness tracking (certs, vouchers, outcomes, throughput).
  • Outcome: Improved visibility into partner readiness and increased throughput across AMER/EMEA/APJ.

04 — Hexed & Hooped (Creative Systems + Productized Craft)

Brand Ops • Production Workflow • Digital Products
  • Context: Built an independent creative brand spanning physical goods and digital products.
  • Problem: Creative work doesn’t scale without process (intake, production, QA, fulfillment, launches).
  • Approach: Designed lightweight systems for commissions intake, batch planning, asset management, and release cadence.
  • Outcome: Consistent output across physical + digital offerings while maintaining quality and brand coherence.
